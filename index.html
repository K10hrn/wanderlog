Restaurant Wishlist
Kikunoi Honten — kaiseki, must book 3 months ahead. Den (Tokyo detour?). Mizai in Kyoto. Ramen at Ichiran solo booth. Dotonbori takoyaki from Kukuru. Nishiki Market street food crawl.
DiningMar 10
Temple Strategy
Go early — before 8am to beat crowds. Buy Kyoto pass for unlimited subway. Fushimi Inari: sunrise is magical and free. Ryoan-ji rock garden is worth the early start. Skip Kinkaku-ji on weekends.
PlanningMar 12
Useful Japanese Phrases
Sumimasen — excuse me / sorry. Arigatou gozaimasu — thank you. Ikura desu ka? — how much? Eigo ga hanasemasu ka? — do you speak English? Toire wa doko desu ka? — where's the toilet?
LanguageMar 14
Cherry Blossom Tips
Peak bloom typically Apr 5–15 in Kyoto. Best spots: Maruyama Park (no fencing!), Philosopher's Path, Hirano Shrine (lesser-known). Bring a picnic for hanami. Check Japan Meteorological Corporation forecasts.
